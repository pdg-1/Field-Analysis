Pricing Models & Benchmarks

Subscription-based models dominate, with tiers catering to different needs. Enterprise pricing is typically custom.

Common Pricing Structures

  • User-Based Subscriptions: Per user/month, role-based (dispatcher, technician).
  • Tiered Packages: Basic to Enterprise with increasing features.
  • Module-Based: Pay for specific FSM/EAM modules (common for large ERPs).
  • Resource-Based: E.g., per technician for optimization engines.
  • Login-Based: For infrequent users like contractors.
